# Privacy Policy for Lion's Notes

*Last updated: March 24, 2026*

---

## 1. Information We Collect

Lion's Notes collects the following information when you use our app:

- **Account information** — name and email address provided through sign-in
- **User-generated content** — tasks, checklists, habits, and notes you create
- **App preferences** — theme, language, and settings stored locally on your device

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

- Provide and sync your tasks and habits across sessions
- Enable shared checklists and live collaboration features
- Maintain your account and app preferences

We do **not** use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

---

## 3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Convex cloud infrastructure hosted in the EU (eu-west-1). All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections.

## 5. Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below. We will process your request within 30 days.

## 6. Children's Privacy

Lion's Notes is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
